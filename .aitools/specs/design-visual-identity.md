# Chrono Album – Visual Identity

Retro-modern design: primarily modern and simple, with warm nostalgic undertones.

## Color Palette

### Light Mode

- **Background**: Warm off-white (`#F8F6F1`)
- **Surfaces**: `#F3F0E8` → `#EBE7DC` → `#E0DAC9`
- **Text**: Soft black (`#2C2C2C`), secondary (`#6B6560`)
- **Accent**: Terracotta (`#C45B28`)

### Dark Mode

- **Background**: Warm dark (`#1A1816`) – never cold black
- **Surfaces**: `#252220` → `#322E2A` → `#403A35`
- **Text**: Warm white (`#F0EBE5`), secondary (`#A89F94`)
- **Accent**: Terracotta bright (`#D97B4A`)

## Typography

- **Headings & Body**: Alike (transitional serif) – warm, nostalgic
- **Dates/metadata**: Smaller, lighter weight, secondary color

## Subtle Nostalgic Touches

- Gentle rounded corners on photos (4-8px)
- Very soft shadows (like prints resting on paper)
- Slight cream tint on image hovers
- Warm background throughout – never cold white
- Subtle grain texture (barely visible CSS noise)

## Layout Principles

- Generous padding and whitespace
- Simple grid, consistent spacing
- Photos as hero – UI steps back
- Minimal chrome, maximum content
- Year groupings with thin horizontal rules

## Interactions

- Quiet transitions (opacity-based, not flashy)
- No complex animations

## Avoid

- Decorative corners, borders, stamps
- Skeuomorphic textures (torn paper, wax seals)
- Multiple accent colors
- Heavy drop shadows

## Feel

Like a well-designed modern gallery that happens to be warm – not a costume party.
